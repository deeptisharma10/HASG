ABOUT OUR SERVICES

HAFA ADAI & WELCOME…

We are Hafa Adai
Specialist Group

Hafa Adai Specialist Group (HASG), is a Multi-Specialty Medical facility that consists of physician offices, physician clinic and an outpatient procedure operating room suite (OR). HASG provides multiple surgical care and services.

Trusted throughout Guam, Dr. Kia Rahmani is highly experienced as a colorectal surgeon and in the treatment of hemorrhoids. Along with his colleagues and their specialties, they offer a wide range of treatment options for patients requiring general surgery.
